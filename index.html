Scarlet Blinks Aromatherapy Pvt Ltd introduces NauseaPlast, a pioneering solution dedicated to revolutionizing nausea management while fostering economic empowerment for tribal communities in India.

Nausea remains a widespread issue affecting individuals across diverse demographics, stemming from triggers such as motion sickness, pregnancy, medical treatments, and various health conditions. Unfortunately, access to natural, effective remedies remains limited.

  • Determined to improve the lives of many!

    Enter NauseaPlast, poised to tackle this challenge head-on by leveraging the rich knowledge of medicinal herbs found within tribal communities. These communities possess invaluable expertise in antiemetic herbs, yet often struggle to translate this knowledge into sustainable livelihoods.

  • Not just a medicine!

    Our vision is twofold: first, to provide innovative solutions for nausea management that are both effective and accessible to all. Second, to empower tribal communities, particularly focusing on women farmers, through agrofarming initiatives.

  • Our Science

    NauseaPlast harnesses the science of aromatherapy and neural modulation to provide effective relief from nausea and discomfort. When triggered by stimuli such as motion sickness or pregnancy, the brain's neural circuitry, including the brainstem and vagus nerve, initiates nausea sensations. NauseaPlast's patches contain natural volatile organic biomolecules, such as zingiberene found in ginger extract, known for their antiemetic properties. These biomolecules interact with receptors in the olfactory system, sending signals to the brain that modulate the activity of the nausea-inducing neural pathways. By interrupting these signals, NauseaPlast mitigates nausea symptoms and restores balance to the body. Through this mechanism, NauseaPlast offers a safe, non-pharmacological solution that targets the root cause of nausea, providing holistic relief for individuals seeking natural alternatives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our FAQ hub for answers to common queries and beyond!

How does NauseaPlast work?

NauseaPlast harnesses the power of natural aromatherapy to provide relief from nausea triggers like motion sickness, pregnancy, and medical treatments.

What are the key ingredients in NauseaPlast?

NauseaPlast contains a blend of natural extracts from medicinal plants known for their anti-nausea properties.

How do I use NauseaPlast?

Simply peel off the backing and apply NauseaPlast to a clean, dry area of the skin, such as the wrist.

How long does NauseaPlast last?

Each NauseaPlast patch provides up to 8 hours of continuous relief from nausea.

Are there any side effects associated with NauseaPlast?

NauseaPlast is made from natural ingredients and is unlikely to cause any adverse reactions. However, individuals with sensitive skin should perform a patch test before use.

Can NauseaPlast be used alongside medication?

Yes, NauseaPlast can be used alongside medication. However, it's always best to consult with a healthcare professional before combining treatments.
